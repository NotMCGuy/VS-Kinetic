# VS Kinetic

*Now featuring consequences.*

---

## What is this?

VS Kinetic is an addon for Valkyrien Skies that introduces kinetic systems with one small but important addition:

**things can fail.**

Not in a polite, “that didn’t work” sort of way — more in a  
> “that used to be your engine”  
sort of way.

If you’ve ever wanted your ships to behave less like floating bricks and more like actual machines under stress, you’re in the right place.

---

## What does it do?

VS Kinetic adds mechanical realism (or at least something *resembling* it) to your ships by introducing:

- **Part failure under stress**  
- **Crash physics** that actually mean something  
- **Mechanical strain and consequences**  
- **Kinetic systems that don’t just magically behave forever**

In other words: your builds now have limits. You can ignore them — briefly.

---

## Features

- **Kinetic Components with Failure States**  
  Parts can and will break if you push them too far. Revolutionary concept.

- **Stress & Load Simulation**  
  Overload your systems and they won’t quietly tolerate it anymore.

- **Crash Physics**  
  Impacts are no longer a mild inconvenience. Your ship will respond… enthusiastically.

- **Structural Consequences**  
  Poor design choices now come with visual feedback. Usually in several pieces.

- **Dynamic Mechanical Behaviour**  
  Systems react to movement, force, and your questionable engineering decisions.

---

## Requirements

You will need:
- Valkyrien Skies  
- Forge  
- A willingness to accept that failure is now part of the gameplay

Strongly recommended:
- Backups  
- Redundancy in your designs  
- Lower expectations  

---

## Installation

1. Install Forge  
2. Install Valkyrien Skies  
3. Drop VS Kinetic into your mods folder  
4. Launch the game  
5. Brace yourself  

---

## How to Use

1. Build your ship  
2. Add kinetic systems  
3. Apply power  
4. Add more power than you should  
5. Observe

Possible outcomes include:
- Smooth operation (rare, but exciting)
- Gradual mechanical failure (educational)
- Immediate catastrophic disassembly (honest)

---

## Design Philosophy

This mod operates on a simple principle:

> If it *can* break, it eventually *will*.

You are encouraged to:
- Design with redundancy  
- Manage stress properly  
- Think ahead for once  

Or don’t — the mod will happily demonstrate why you should have.

---

## Known Issues

- Parts may fail exactly when you need them not to  
- Crashes may escalate faster than expected  
- Systems under stress tend to express their feelings violently  
- Your “perfect design” probably isn’t  

---

## FAQ

**Q: Why did my engine fail mid-flight?**  
A: Because it was under load. That’s how loads work.

**Q: Why did my ship fall apart on impact?**  
A: Because it hit something. At speed.

**Q: Can I disable failure?**  
A: You can also remove the point of the mod, yes.

---

## Final Notes

VS Kinetic exists to answer the question:  
> “What if physics actually mattered?”

It turns out the answer is:
> “You’ll need a better design.”

If it breaks, that’s not a bug. That’s feedback.
